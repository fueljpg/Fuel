How It Works

Fuel tracks the three pillars together and coaches the relationship between them.

  1. 1
    Log food fast. AI Calorie Scanner turns a barcode or label into accurate macros in seconds.
  2. 2
    Plan your training. Gym AI builds programs for strength, hypertrophy, or endurance.
  3. 3
    Track the work. Auto-log sets/reps/RPE & detect PRs without extra taps.
  4. 4
    Sync sleep. Connect your smartwatch; Fuel derives a daily recovery score.
  5. 5
    Review & adapt. Weekly view shows how Nutrition × Training × Sleep interact—and adjusts targets.
